Junghyun Kim

I'm an undergraduate at Yonsei University, majoring in Business Administration. My academic focus lies in leveraging AI and technology for social innovation and consumer behavior analysis. I am particularly interested in exploring how Generative AI and Web Development can create impactful solutions that address real-world challenges.

Currently, I am involved in projects that focus on improving accessibility and enhancing user experience through innovative platforms. For example, I developed a web platform for disability mobility rights, and I also contributed to the creation of a real-time seat tracking system as part of the Uni.flow initiative at Yonsei University.

Previously, I co-founded a Youth Mentorship Program aimed at helping middle school students explore their interests and discover potential career paths. This experience has strengthened my passion for creating technology-driven solutions that not only innovate but also provide value to the community.
