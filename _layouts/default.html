I'm an undergraduate at Yonsei University, majoring in Business Administration and AI & Business (Convergence Advanced Major).

I believe that innovation starts by addressing the discomforts I and those around me experience in daily life. That's why I put considerable effort into understanding and analyzing users' minds. Now, I am currently strengthening my foundation by taking courses in Consumer Behavior and Strategic Management, studying UX design methodologies in a club, and building an understanding of technical skill sets.

Previously, I was involved in projects that captured consumer complaints and introduced innovative solutions based on web products. For example, I developed a web platform for disability mobility rights, and I also contributed to the creation of a real-time seat tracking system as part of the Uni.flow initiative at Yonsei University.
