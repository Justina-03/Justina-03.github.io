Real-Time Seat Tracking at Uni.flow

Junghyun Kim, Doyoung Kweon, Yoonseo Jung, Hyunjin Kang

🗓️ August 2023 ~ March 2024

Our team initially started by recommending campus spaces and providing detailed information, focusing on introducing hidden spots within the school. We concentrated on creating content and uploading it on Notion and Instagram. However, later on, we realized that the main issue students faced was the inability to access real-time information. As a result, we collaborated with the development team to create a device that could address this problem. We even got the chance to install these devices in places like Hankyung Hall and Matnasaem and launched the information on the web. During this process, I contributed by conceptualizing the website where the information would be uploaded and visualizing it using Figma, while also overseeing general promotion and contacting stakeholders.

AI Convergence Idea Contest

Minju Kim, Junghyun Kim, Jaeeun Lee, Jihoon Woo

🗓️ December 2023

Our team predicted AI issues that could arise in ten years and devised onboarding strategies for each domain. We raised concerns about the monopolization of data power by centralized AI agent systems and proposed a decentralized AI solution that combines concepts from Bitcoin and AI as a way to address this. Additionally, we suggested efforts in international politics, law, and education to mitigate the drawbacks and strengthen the advantages of decentralized AI, using practical examples like establishing legal regulations for unstructured information, AI governance, and creating participatory, hands-on curriculums for each domain.
